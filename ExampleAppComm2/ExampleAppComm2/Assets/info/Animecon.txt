Welcome to Anime 2016
Anime 2016 will be from 10 to 12 June in the World Forum The Hague. The theme will be "All aboard!"

You can order your tickets and book your hotel.

Anime 2016 is the largest and longest running festival in the Netherlands for fans of Asian games, movies, comics, food, cosplay and other popular culture.

Starting on Friday the 10th of June at 14:00 the continuous event program will entertain you until Sunday at 17:00.
